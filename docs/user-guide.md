# fofafu User Guide

fofafu is a private community platform for foster families. This guide covers everything you can do on the site.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an account

Go to the **Register** page and enter your email address and a password. After you submit the form, fofafu sends a verification email to that address. Click the link in the email to activate your account — you cannot log in until your email is verified.

### Logging in

Visit the **Login** page, enter your verified email and password, and you're in. You'll land on the home dashboard.

### Resetting your password

On the Login page, click **Forgot password** and enter your email. If an account exists for that email, you'll receive a reset link (valid for 1 hour). Follow the link to set a new password. For security, fofafu does not confirm whether an email is registered.

### Changing your password

When you're already logged in, you can change your password from your family profile settings.

## Home Dashboard

The home dashboard has three columns:

- **Left — Your family card.** Shows your family's name, avatar, and a shortcut to your profile.
- **Center — Announcements feed.** The latest posts from the community, with a composer at the top so you can post right away.
- **Right — Community sidebar.** Recent community members you can browse.

---

## Announcements Feed

### Browsing posts

The feed at `/feed` shows announcements from all families, newest first. Each post shows the author's family name and avatar. Scroll down to load older posts.

### Creating a post

Click the composer at the top of the feed. Type your message. You can optionally attach an image (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF; max 5 MB) using the image button. Click **Post** to publish.

### Reacting to a post

Click a reaction emoji below any post to add your reaction. Click again to remove it.

### Commenting

Click **Comment** on a post to open the comment thread. Type your reply and press **Send**. To edit a comment you wrote, click the edit icon next to it — the editor opens inline and saves when you confirm. All comments show an "(edited)" label if they've been changed.

### Editing or deleting your own post

Posts you authored show an edit and a delete icon. Editing opens an inline editor. Deleting asks for confirmation before removing the post permanently.

### Reply coach

Before you publish a comment, fofafu may gently flag language that could feel harmful and offer a suggested rewrite. You can accept the suggestion, edit it further, or post your original text — the choice is always yours.

## Family Profiles

### Your family profile

Go to **My Family** (or `/family/me`) to see and edit your family profile:

- **Display name** — the name shown next to your posts and in search.
- **Bio** — a short description of your family.
- **Kid count** — how many foster children are currently in your home.
- **Avatar** — upload a photo (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF; max 5 MB) or use the auto-generated initials avatar.

Changes take effect immediately.

### Viewing another family's profile

Click a family name anywhere on the site to open their public profile. You'll see their display name, bio, and recent posts. Their kid count is hidden for privacy. A **Send a message** button links directly to a DM thread with them.

---

## Direct Messages

### Thread list

The **Messages** page (`/messages`) shows all your DM threads. Each row displays the other family's name, a preview of the last message, and an unread badge if there are new messages.

### Reading and sending messages

Click a thread to open it. Messages appear oldest at the top. Type in the composer at the bottom and press **Send**. Messages are marked as read automatically when you open the thread.

---

## Community Search

Go to **Search** (`/search`) to find other families. Type any combination of name, bio text, city, or state. Results appear as cards you can click to view that family's profile.

---

## Keyboard Navigation & Accessibility

fofafu is designed to be fully usable with a keyboard and screen reader:

- All interactive elements are reachable by **Tab** and activatable with **Enter** or **Space**.
- Focus is reset to the main content area on every page navigation so screen reader users always start at the top of the new page.
- Color contrast meets WCAG 2.2 AA on all pages.
- All images and icons have descriptive labels for assistive technology.
